# Brand Positioning Evals
Each eval is binary Y/N. Pass threshold: 8/10 outputs.

- [ ] Does the niche name a specific sub-market (not a broad category like "marketing")?
- [ ] Does differentiation state what makes this brand the ONLY choice for a specific customer?
- [ ] Does the origin story include a specific turning point moment (not just a chronological journey)?
- [ ] Does tone of voice include 3+ specific language rules (not vague adjectives like "professional")?
- [ ] Is the bio 1-2 sentences maximum with at least one concrete proof point (credential or result)?
- [ ] Does differentiation frame the brand in "only we" terms (not a feature list)?
