# Content Engine Evals
Each eval is binary Y/N. Pass threshold: 8/10 outputs.

- [ ] Does the 4-week calendar have at least 5 specific posts mapped to platforms per week?
- [ ] Do newsletter drafts follow APAG format (Attention, Perspective, Action, Go deeper)?
- [ ] Do post hooks have specific angles (not generic "save time" or "get results" claims)?
- [ ] Is the content waterfall mapped with clear repurposing logic per platform?
- [ ] Does content reference the customer avatar's specific pain points by name?
- [ ] Are all claims backed by specific numbers or metrics (not vague "improve" or "better")?
