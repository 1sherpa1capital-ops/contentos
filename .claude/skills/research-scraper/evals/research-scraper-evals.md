# Research Scraper Evals
Each eval is binary Y/N. Pass threshold: 8/10 outputs.

- [ ] Are there at least 10 emotionally charged phrases with source attribution?
- [ ] Are there at least 5 verbatim complaints with source URLs?
- [ ] Are sources from at least 2 different platforms (Reddit, LinkedIn, reviews)?
- [ ] Is the output saved to output/research-raw.md (not just displayed)?
- [ ] Are all quotes verbatim (no paraphrasing without [bracketed attribution])?
- [ ] Are at least 3 trigger events identified with customer quotes?
